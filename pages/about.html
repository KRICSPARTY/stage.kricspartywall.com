About us

With a team of experienced surveyors, we specialise in providing comprehensive Party Wall surveying services.

We understand the intricacies of the Party Wall Act and are dedicated to ensuring that your construction projects comply with legal requirements while minimising disputes.

Why Choose KRICS?

Expertise: We bring years of combined experience in the industry. We stay abreast of the latest regulations and possess the knowledge to navigate complex Party Wall scenarios.
Client-Centric Approach:We prioritise clear communication and collaboration with our clients. You are guaranteed to have a direct contact with the surveyor. From initial consultations to the completion of the project, we strive to keep you informed and involved every step of the way.
Comprehensive Services: Whether you are a property owner planning construction work, an adjoining owner concerned about potential impacts, or a developer seeking compliance, KRICS offers a full suite of Party Wall surveying services to address your specific requirements.
Legal Compliance: We have a deep understanding of the Party Wall Act and relevant regulations, ensuring that your projects are conducted in accordance with the law.
Peace of Mind:Choosing KRICS means choosing reliability and professionalism. We work diligently to anticipate and resolve potential issues, providing you with peace of mind throughout the Party Wall process.
Cost effective: With our lean structure, we are committed to maintaining low fee levels not normally offered for the exceptionally high level of service given.
